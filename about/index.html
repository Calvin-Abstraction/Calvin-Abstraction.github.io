About

Our Mission

We exist to serve the community of Calvin students interested in computer science.

We currently serve in a few ways.

  1. We provide opportunities by hosting talks and promoting jobs and internships for the community to grow.
  2. We provide resources in the forms of people, hardware, software.
  3. We provide a platform to help grow the C.S. community at Calvin.

Joining Abstraction

Welcome! Other than subscribing to our emailing list, there are no formal membership procedures to join Abstraction. If you are interested in computer science or computing and are a student at Calvin (or formerly at Calvin), you are free to join.
